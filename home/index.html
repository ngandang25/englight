Mother

Nghĩa: Mẹ

Câu ví dụ:

  • My mother is a wonderful cook.

    Mẹ tôi là một đầu bếp tuyệt vời.

  • I called my mother to wish her a happy birthday.

    Tôi đã gọi điện cho mẹ để chúc mừng sinh nhật bà ấy.

Đoạn văn hôm nay

My family is small. I have a mom, a dad, and a little sister. My mom is a teacher. My dad is a doctor. My sister is very cute. We live in a house with a big garden. We often eat dinner together. We play games on weekends. My family is very important to me. I love them very much. We are happy together.
